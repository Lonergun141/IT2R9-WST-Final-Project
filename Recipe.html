Regular Burger

Dish Type

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Cook Duration: 20mins
  • Number of Ingredients: 5
  • >Serving: 1
  • Ratings: 4.5

Nutrients

Calories

Fat

Cholesterol

Sodium

Protein

Ingredients

Name Amount
Burger Bun 1 pc
Lettuce 1 pc
Onion Rings 2 pcs
Beef Patty 1 pc
Bacon Mayonnaise 1 cup

Method

  1. Oil your grill or a cast-iron pan, and grill or sear the beef patty as well as your burger bun around 54 degrees Celsius. Cook them to your desired doneness.
  2. Once the beef patty is ready, add onion rings on top and let it sit on the patty for a couple of minutes.
  3. Add the lettuce next and make sure it is already washed and cleaned.
  4. Spread the bacon mayonnaise generously on the base toasted bun.
  5. After making your burger, serve it on a plate with your desired dips on the side. Enjoy your meal!
